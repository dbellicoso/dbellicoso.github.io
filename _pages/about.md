Hi! I am Dario, a Research Scientist, Project Lead, and Controls Lead at the <a href="https://rai-inst.com/">RAI Institute</a> in Zurich, Switzerland. Before joining the Institute, I was a Senior Staff Research Scientist and Technical Lead at <a href="https://bostondynamics.com/">Boston Dynamics</a>, where I developed algorithms for motion planning, control, and reinforcement learning. I received my Ph.D. from the <a href="http://www.rsl.ethz.ch">Robotic Systems Lab</a> of <a href="https://www.ethz.ch/en.html">ETH Zurich</a> focusing on motion planning and control for multi-limbed walking robots. During my research, I developed algorithms that allow <a href="https://youtu.be/m1-s8iOJaI4">ANYmal</a>, a torque-controlled quadrupedal robot, to walk, run, jump, and open doors.
